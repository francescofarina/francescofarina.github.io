About

Ciao

I am an AI/ML research scientist at GSK.ai. My current research concerns geometric deep learning (especially creating graph architectures that are able to exploit symmetries and equivariance in data), causal discovery through graph networks and uncertainty estimation.

Previously, my research has focused on distributed (multi-agent) optimization, machine learning, estimation and control. In this area, I have published works in prominent journals and conferences, including Automatica (Elsevier), IEEE Transactions on Automatic Control, IEEE Transactions on Neural Networks and Learning Systems and Plos ONE.
